Forms

There are two types of forms:

  • List Forms, and
  • Details Forms.

Forms may also be either:

  • locked – in browsing mode, or
  • unlocked – in editing mode.

When a form is locked, its record(s) cannot be changed using the keyboard. Use appropriate "Manage"-items from menu to start forms in editing mode. You may also use F2 to toggle between locked/unlocked modes at any time.

In List Forms...

F2 toggles between locked/unlocked mode.

In unlocked mode, you can use Insert/Delete keys to add/remove records.

To open search panel press Ctrl+F. When searching, you may delimit words with character '#' spaces; for example, enter #45# to find records containing word 45. This slightly differs from the TextUI application where character Space is used as a word delimiter.

In Details Forms...

F2 toggles between locked/unlocked mode.

To open corresponding list form and start searching, press Ctrl+F.

How to Rent a New Item?

  1. Open customer list form (either Browse, Find or Manage customers)
  2. Select current customer
  3. Press Shift+F9
